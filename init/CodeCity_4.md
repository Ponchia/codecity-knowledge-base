# CodeCity and city metaphor for software visualization: A comprehensive bibliography

The city metaphor for software visualization, pioneered by Richard Wettel's CodeCity project at USI Lugano, has generated **over 400 publications** and spawned numerous tools and implementations. This resource list covers the foundational research, subsequent academic work, implementations, and community resources.

---
